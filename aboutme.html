About Me

Welcome to my portfolio !!!

My journey into data analytics began at Pidilite Industries Ltd., a chemical manufacturing company, where I started as a Research Analyst. This role ignited my passion for leveraging data to drive insights and support operational and strategic decision-making.

Transitioning to Syngene Intl Ltd., I took on the role of Business Analyst within the pharmaceutical domain. Here, I gained valuable experience in utilizing CRM systems and advanced analytics to optimize sales strategies and enhance operational efficiencies.

Building on my industry experience, I pursued a Master of Science degree in Business Analytics from Santa Clara University's Leavey School of Business. This program equipped me with advanced skills in Python, SQL, and Tableau, preparing me for more specialized roles in data analysis.

Following my education, I joined Oracle Inc. as a Data Analyst focused on marketing strategy. In this role, I collaborated closely with sales teams to analyze data and provide actionable insights that supported marketing initiatives and improved decision-making processes.

Currently, I am freelancing as a Data Analyst, where I continue to apply my skills and expertise across various projects. This role allows me to leverage my analytical skills to extract valuable insights and contribute to strategic decision-making in diverse contexts.

Driven by a passion for continuous learning and a commitment to leveraging data for business success, I am eager to explore new opportunities and contribute to innovative projects in the field of data analytics.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with you to discuss potential collaborations and opportunities to apply data-driven solutions effectively. Let's harness the power of data together to achieve impactful outcomes!
